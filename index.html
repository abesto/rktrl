What is this?

A roguelike game I'm building by following along the outstanding tutorial at https://bfnightly.bracketproductions.com/rustbook/ and tweaking things as I go.

It's also Rust compiled to WASM, which is pretty amazing.

The source is at abesto/rktrl.

Fine, what can I do?

  • Move around with the VI keys, or arrows, or the numpad
  • Hit monsters by walking into them
  • Pick up items with g
  • Open your inventory with i to pick an item to use. Ranged targeting happens with the mouse.
  • Pick an item to drop with d
  • Hover over things with your mouse for a tooltip
